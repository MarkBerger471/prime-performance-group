Our Story

From Vision to Reality

How a passion for performance and a gap in the Thai supplement market sparked a brand built on integrity.

"We didn't start Prime Performance Group because we wanted to sell supplements. We started it because we were tired of buying bad ones."

— The Founders

Chapter 1

Identifying the Gap

We noticed that Thailand's supplement market was flooded with under-dosed, poorly labeled products. Athletes and fitness enthusiasts deserved better — Western-grade quality without the import markups.

Walking through supplement stores in Bangkok, the pattern was clear: flashy packaging, proprietary blends hiding minimal dosages, and prices inflated by unnecessary middlemen. Something had to change.

Chapter 2

Research & Formulation

We partnered with world-class formulators and invested in clinical-dose research. Every ingredient is chosen for its evidence base, not its marketing appeal.

Months of research. Hundreds of formulation iterations. We studied every peer-reviewed paper we could find and consulted with sports nutritionists, exercise physiologists, and competitive athletes.

Chapter 3

Launch in Thailand

Prime Performance Group launched with a core lineup of pre-workouts, protein, and recovery formulas — and the response from the Thai fitness community was immediate.

Our first batch sold out in 72 hours. Not because of hype marketing, but because athletes could finally read the label and see real doses. Word spread fast.

Chapter 4

Today & Beyond

Now serving athletes across Thailand with an expanding product line and a growing community. This is just the beginning.

With over 200 gym partnerships, 50+ athletes in our ambassador program, and a product line that keeps growing — PPG is becoming the gold standard for supplements in Thailand. And we're only getting started.
