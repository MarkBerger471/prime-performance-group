Built for Those

Premium Supplements,

Prime Performance Group is a premium supplement brand based in Bangkok, Thailand. We combine Western formulation standards with local accessibility, delivering products that serious athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and health-conscious individuals can trust.

Every product is third-party tested, transparently labeled, and designed to deliver measurable results. No proprietary blends. No fillers. Just science and performance.

We believe the supplement industry in Southeast Asia needed a shake-up. Too many under-dosed, poorly labeled products. Athletes and fitness enthusiasts deserved better — and that's exactly what we set out to build.
